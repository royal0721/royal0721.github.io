About Me

Hello, my name is Kika

嗨，我是 Kika

I’m a beginner in cybersecurity, fueled by curiosity and a love for clear thinking.
I may not be advanced (yet!), but I truly enjoy “breaking down concepts”, taking notes, and sharing what I learn in a way that’s easy to understand.

我是一位剛踏入 cybersecurity 世界的新手，靠著好奇心和整理腦邏輯在前進。
雖然還不算厲害，但我真的很喜歡把學到的東西整理清楚，畫成圖、寫成文，幫助自己也幫助別人理解。

What I’m Learning 我正在學的主題

  • Linux privilege escalation & system enumeration
    Linux 權限提升與系統資訊枚舉

  • Web vulnerabilities and beginner CTF practice
    網頁漏洞與 CTF 新手題練習

  • How to turn technical notes into learning maps
    如何把技術筆記變成一目了然的學習地圖

What I Love Doing 我喜歡的事

  • Making neat, visual notes out of messy docs
    把混亂的資訊整理成清楚有圖的筆記

  • Explaining things the way I wish someone explained to me
    用「我當初希望有人這樣教我」的方式講解

  • Being honest about what I don’t know (yet!)
    誠實面對自己還不會的地方，然後勇敢補起來

About Me 個人小簡介

I love playing guitar, doing Pilates, and drinking unsweetened tea.
I’m an INFJ — quiet on the outside, but always thinking and creating on the inside.

我喜歡彈吉他、做皮拉提斯、喝無糖紅。
我是 INFJ，看起來安靜，腦袋卻一直轉不停。
我想成為一個能靠實力自由生活，也能溫暖身邊人的人。

Manifesting 願景與夢想

I hope one day I can give a talk on something I truly understand — not because I rushed, but because I showed up, every day.
And if you’re also in cybersecurity, I’d love to learn and grow with you.

有一天，我希望能站上台分享真正內化的知識，
不是因為我天生聰明，而是因為我每天都有好好努力。
如果你也在學 cybersecurity，我們可以一起變強！
